Gleba

To combat spoilage:
  • fully-grown trees are overproduced, and harvested fruit is kept only in small quantities,
  • assembly lines are kept short in parallel biochamber groups, and
  • excess is burned.
In case spoilage does happen, inserters at common failure points remove it to be burned. A small amount of excess nutrients are constantly fed to the main nutrient providers, in case their own nutrients spoil. This also allows automatic start-up for new biochamber groups. Tesla turrets are included in any biochamber group that deals with eggs.
Gleba was powered by nuclear until fusion became availble.
Gleba is protected by a perimeter of Tesla turrets. When they became available, artillery were added.
